OUR STORY

Who We Are

NCBS was founded on November 16, 2022 in Jinja City, Uganda.
It embarked on a mission to empower educated graduate youth with essential skills in Accounting and IT.
Our sole director, Matthijs van De Wetering envisioned a world where businesses thrive through efficient financial management and cutting-edge technology.
  • Mission:
    To empower Ugandan youth with world-class digital and
    professional skills while providing reliable, high-quality
    remote business support to companies across Europe.
  • Vision:
    To become the leading bridge between African talent
    and European business needs, demonstrating that
    remote work can be a powerful tool for economic empowerment and international collaboration.

How we work

  • Hire
    We engage in a thorough consultation with candidates to understand their unique needs, aspirations, and skills. Our team evaluates candidates based on their technical capabilities, intellect, commitment, and potential.
  • Train
    NCBS nurtures talent and ensures readiness through comprehensive training in various domains and continuous learning to stay updated on industry trends and technologies.
  • Deploy
    With remote deployment, experts integrate into workflows globally. They maintain high-quality service delivery and prioritize customer satisfaction.
