Entrepreneurship and Sustainability

Olivia
03 Comment

Nile Centre Business Support is proud to have participated in the International Summer School on Entrepreneurship and Sustainability, hosted by Stellenbosch University from 20–28 January 2026.

This intensive program, developed as a Think Tank and Project Case Study Challenge, is a joint initiative with MCI | The Entrepreneurial School® (Austria), Makerere University (Uganda), UNESCO Futures Capability Centre, SU LaunchLab, and Nile Centre Business Support.

What actually happens during the International Summer School?

It brought together 32 students from Uganda, Nigeria, Zimbabwe, South Africa, Germany and Austria for a collaborative learning experience focused on addressing complex sustainability challenges through entrepreneurship.

Our Director,Matthijs van de Wetering and two Nile Centre Business Support talent Olivia Ajambo and Nabakka Brendah participated in interactive lectures, workshops and multidisciplinary group case studies, gaining insights in futures literacy, business development, innovation ecosystems, value proposition design and intercultural collaboration. The program emphasized critical reflection, encouraging participants to adopt pluriversal approaches to problem solving and understand the local and global impact of entrepreneurial action.

Such exposure strengthens Nile Centre Business Support`s mission to develop globally competent professionals capable of supporting businesses and driving sustainable economic growth. Partner with Nile Centre Business Support to bring global expertise and innovative solutions to your business.
